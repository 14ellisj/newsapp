Chaos as driver hits parked car and flips onto roof in Sheffield crash
A woman has been arrested and she remains in police custody

A woman has been arrested on suspicion of drink driving after her car crashed into a parked car and landed on its roof in Sheffield.

The woman, 37, was arrested after crashing her red Ford Fiesta in Dykes Hall Road just before 10.26pm on Wednesday. South Yorkshire Police have kept her in custody. Police closed the road while the Ford Fiesta was cleared from the scene.

A spokesperson for the force said: "We received a call at 10.26pm for reports of a road traffic collision on Dykes Hall Road in Sheffield. It is believed that a red Ford Fiesta collided with a parked car and landed on its roof.

"The driver, a 37 year old woman was arrested on suspicion of driving while over the prescribed limit of alcohol. She remains in police custody. The vehicle was recovered."
